# Scientific Writing

Use this skill when turning accepted lab evidence into prose.

## Writing Rules

- Write from accepted reports and critic verdicts.
- Keep claims tied to evidence.
- Preserve uncertainty, limitations, and negative results.
- Do not introduce new scientific claims while drafting.

## Output Types

- Lab summaries
- Abstracts
- Manuscript sections
- Figure captions
- README-style method summaries
- PI-facing executive summaries

## Report Expectations

- List source reports and critic verdicts used.
- Identify claims included and claims excluded.
- State audience, format, and remaining factual gaps.
